M.Sc. Thesis

Nonlinear Behavior Of Fixed Oﬀshore Platform Under Environmental Loads.

Abstract: The aim of my research was to analyze the behavior and demand of elements of a structure in order to reach an optimized design. For this purpose, I modeled a case study oﬀshore platform using Macro method. Simulating the designed load conditions, this structure was analyzed using Distributed Plasticity method as well as the time history dynamic loading. Various software including SACS and OpenSees were utilized to perform nonlinear analysis. However, to accomplish the project I developed a software using C# programming language which decodes SACS complicated 3D model and remodel it by TCL programming somehow can be usable in OpenSees platform for further nonlinear analysis.
